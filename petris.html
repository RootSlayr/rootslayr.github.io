PETris

PET Scan Appointment Booking and Management Platform

PETris is a full-stack web application designed to streamline PET scan bookings and improve patient management. The platform provides a secure and responsive interface, enhancing the efficiency of medical imaging service providers. [cite: 8]

Features

  • Online appointment booking for patients
  • Automated appointment reminders and notifications
  • Secure patient data management
  • Reporting and analytics for tracking appointments and patient data
  • User-friendly interface for both patients and administrators

Technical Details

  • Role: Full-stack Developer
  • Technologies: C#, ASP.NET MVC, MS SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Achievements: Increased revenue by 33% and improved booking efficiency, saving an average of 10 minutes per patient.
